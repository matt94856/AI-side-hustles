Lesson 1: AI Image Generation Fundamentals

Understanding AI Image Generation

AI image generators create visuals based on text descriptions (prompts) by analyzing patterns from millions of images they were trained on. These tools have revolutionized visual creation, allowing anyone to produce professional-quality images without traditional artistic skills.

  • Text-to-image models convert your written descriptions into visual output
  • Different AI models have distinct aesthetic styles and capabilities
  • Results depend heavily on the quality and specificity of your prompts
  • Generated images can be further refined through editing or additional prompts

The Art of Prompt Engineering for Images

Creating effective prompts is crucial for getting the images you want. Here's how to structure them:

Basic Prompt Structure:

[Subject] in [Environment], [Lighting], [Perspective], [Style], [Quality]

Example:
"A futuristic cyberpunk city at sunset, neon lights, aerial view, digital art, highly detailed, 8K"

Key elements to include in your prompts:

  1. Subject: What/who is the main focus?
  2. Setting/Environment: Where is the subject located?
  3. Lighting: What kind of lighting sets the mood?
  4. Style: What artistic style should the image have?
  5. Perspective: What viewpoint or camera angle?
  6. Quality Modifiers: Terms like "detailed," "high resolution," "8K," etc.
  7. Color Palette: Specific colors or mood you want
  8. Artists/Influences: Reference specific artists or styles

Common Image Styles and Modifiers

Photorealistic

Looks like a real photograph with natural lighting and details

Digital Art

Clean, polished artwork created digitally with clear lines

Watercolor

Soft, flowing style mimicking traditional watercolor painting

3D Render

Computer-generated 3D model with defined shapes and lighting

Working with AI Image Tools: Step-by-Step

  1. Select Your Tool: Choose based on your needs (artistic quality, realism, etc.)
  2. Write Your Prompt: Be detailed but concise
  3. Generate Initial Images: Create several versions
  4. Refine Your Prompt: Adjust based on initial results
  5. Create Variations: Use the tool's features to explore alternatives
  6. Upscale/Enhance: Improve resolution and details
  7. Save and Export: Download in appropriate format

Practical Exercise: Your First AI Images

  1. Tool Selection: Create a free account on DALL-E (via ChatGPT) or another AI image generator
  2. Basic Prompt: Create an image with a simple prompt like "Mountain landscape at sunset"
  3. Enhanced Prompt: Add more details like "Mountain landscape at sunset, golden light, dramatic clouds, wide angle, cinematic, 8K, highly detailed"
  4. Style Experiment: Try the same prompt but add a specific style: "...in the style of watercolor painting"
  5. Compare Results: Note the differences between your basic and enhanced prompts

Ethical and Legal Considerations

  • Check each platform's terms regarding commercial usage rights
  • Be cautious with celebrity likenesses or copyrighted characters
  • Avoid generating misleading or deceptive images
  • Consider disclosing AI-generated imagery when publishing
  • Be aware of potential biases in generated images

Creating Cohesive Visual Sets

One of the advantages of AI tools is the ability to quickly generate multiple related assets that share a consistent style. Here's how to approach this:

  1. Define Your Style: Create a reference image that establishes your desired aesthetic
  2. Use Style Continuity: Incorporate consistent phrases in your prompts ("same style as previous")
  3. Maintain Color Palette: Specify the same color scheme across assets
  4. Create Variations: Generate multiple options of the same concept for selection
  5. Document Successful Prompts: Save prompts that produce good results for future use
Cohesive Set Prompt Template:

"Create a [type of graphic] for [purpose] featuring [subject/theme].
Use a [color palette description] color scheme with a [style] aesthetic.
The graphic should maintain the same visual style as my brand which features [brand style characteristics].
Include space for text that says [text content]."

Example:
"Create a Facebook cover image for a digital marketing agency featuring abstract technology elements.
Use a blue and purple gradient color scheme with a clean, modern aesthetic.
The graphic should maintain the same visual style as my brand which features geometric shapes and subtle grid patterns.
Include space for text that says 'Digital Solutions for Tomorrow's Challenges'."

Social Media Graphics Creation

Best practices for creating social media graphics with AI:

  • Design graphics in sets for carousel posts and multi-platform campaigns

Infographics and Data Visualization

While AI tools can't create complete data visualizations directly from data, they can help with:

  1. Background Elements: Generate visually appealing backgrounds and textures
  2. Thematic Icons: Create consistent icon sets for different data points
  3. Visual Metaphors: Develop images that represent concepts in your data
  4. Layout Inspiration: Generate reference designs for your infographic layout
  5. Final Refinement: Enhance manually created charts and graphs with AI-generated design elements

Adapting AI-Generated Images for Commercial Use

  • Understand the licensing terms of your AI tool (Adobe Firefly and Microsoft Designer are designed for commercial use)
  • Make substantial modifications to generic AI outputs to create unique work
  • Combine multiple AI-generated elements with original design work
  • Consider using AI-generated images as references rather than final assets for commercial projects
  • Document your creative process and modifications for transparency

Creating Videos with AI Avatars

AI avatars provide a quick way to create presenter-led videos without needing to appear on camera yourself:

  1. Script Preparation: Write a conversational script with natural language
  2. Avatar Selection: Choose an avatar that matches your brand and content style
  3. Voice Configuration: Select voice type, language, and speaking style
  4. Background Selection: Choose appropriate backdrop for your video
  5. Visual Elements: Add supporting visuals like images or slides
  6. Review and Generate: Preview and make adjustments before final rendering
Sample Avatar Video Script Structure:

[INTRO - 15-20 seconds]
"Hi there! Welcome to [Channel/Brand Name]. I'm [Avatar Name], and today we're going to explore [Topic].
This video will cover [3-4 key points you'll address]."

[MAIN CONTENT - Organized in sections]
"First, let's talk about [Point 1].
[2-3 sentences explaining Point 1]
[Include direction for visual element: [SHOW IMAGE: description]]

Now, moving on to [Point 2]..."

[CALL TO ACTION - 10-15 seconds]
"If you found this helpful, be sure to [desired action]. And don't forget to [secondary action]."

[OUTRO - 5-10 seconds]
"Thanks for watching, and I'll see you in the next video!"

Text-to-Video Generation Tips

Text-to-video tools like Runway Gen-2 can create impressive video clips from text descriptions:

  • Be specific about camera movement (static, panning, zooming, etc.)
  • Describe the scene, lighting, and atmosphere in detail
  • Specify if you want slow motion, time-lapse, or normal speed
  • Keep clips short (5-10 seconds) for best results
  • Generate multiple variations and select the best
  • Use generated clips as b-roll or supporting content in longer videos

Enhancing Videos with AI Editing Tools

AI can significantly improve your video editing workflow:

  1. Automatic Transcription: Generate accurate subtitles and enable text-based editing
  2. Content-Aware Editing: Remove filler words, uhms, and awkward pauses
  3. Auto-Cutting: Generate cuts and transitions based on content
  4. Music Generation: Create royalty-free background tracks
  5. Voice Enhancement: Improve audio quality and reduce background noise
  6. Visual Enhancement: Upscale resolution, stabilize footage, and improve lighting

Practical Exercise: Create Your First AI Video

  1. Choose Your Platform: Sign up for a free trial of Synthesia, HeyGen, or similar avatar platform
  2. Write a Script: Create a 30-60 second script introducing a topic you're knowledgeable about
  3. Select Avatar & Background: Choose an appropriate presenter and setting for your content
  4. Add Visuals: Incorporate 2-3 supporting images or graphics (AI-generated or stock)
  5. Generate & Review: Create your video and note areas for improvement

Ethical Considerations in AI Video Creation

  • Be mindful of deepfake concerns when using realistic avatars
  • Respect copyright and licensing for any incorporated materials
  • Consider the potential impact on human creators and jobs
  • Follow platform guidelines regarding acceptable content generation

Combining Different AI Video Techniques

For more sophisticated content, combine multiple AI video approaches:

  • Use avatar videos for presenter sections and introductions
  • Incorporate text-to-video generated clips as b-roll footage
  • Enhance with AI-generated animations and graphics
  • Apply AI editing tools for final polish and refinement
