Lesson 1: Understanding AI Consulting

What is AI Consulting?

AI consulting involves helping businesses identify, implement, and optimize artificial intelligence solutions to solve specific business problems. As an AI consultant, you'll guide organizations through their AI transformation journey.

Key Areas of AI Consulting:

  • Strategy Development
  • Solution Architecture
  • Implementation Support
  • Training and Change Management
  • Performance Optimization
